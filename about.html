What we Do

Pilates and Passion os Pilates at-home mat workout that you can take it on your holiday, on your business travel. you can take Pilates anywhere. Each online class has a deliberate to moderate pace suiting a beginner or an excisting practioner. Pilates on the Mat applies the principles of classical method as taught by the founder, Joseph Pilates himself. Pilates is a way to strech and strengthen your body, primarily the muscles of the core, which span from your hips to shoulders.

The standard Pilates system of exercise uses many different pieces of equipment, such as the reformer, cadillac and barrel. But, the mat Pilates series of exercise is performed equipment-free, with the moves adapted to work with just your body and floor mat for cushioning and support

Discover the benefits of this powerful practise that is about core-strengthening, full body development with simple to follow and highly effective, at-home mat workouts. We'll help you create an online practise that is suites to your needs. Join us, and feel the movement of liberation!

Meet the Instructor

Ramya Naren, Internationally certified Pilates Instructor. A graduate of the Pilates Centre, Boulder, Colorado, touded as the Harward of Pilates. She practised under the guidance of the licensed trainer Kavita Prakash at Pilates for wellbeing, Studio, Bangalore. As a former corporate leader and a mother of teenager, Ramya was convinced that there is more to living outside of the human hamster wheel. Join us and discover the mind boday and spirit connection.

‘I do not have a special talent. I am not a gymnast or a soccer player. I do have certain parts of my body that are less strong than the others, similarly, we all have something we can work on and strengthen in each of our bodies. But importantly, I have a sharp mind and a bone or two of resilience.Faith, consistency and the practice of the Pilates movement everyday have empowered me to be in a constant state of ‘becoming’!
